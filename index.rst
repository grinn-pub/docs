Grinn AstraADA-1680
-------------------

The Grinn AstraADA is a compact adapter module that connects the AstraSOM-1680 with the Synaptics Astra Machina Foundation Series demo board, enabling efficient prototyping and validation. It offers high-speed connectivity features like Gigabit Ethernet, HDMI RX, QSPI Flash, and MicroSD support, making it ideal for IoT, industrial, and multimedia use cases.

Grinn AstraEVB-1680
-------------------

Grinn AstraEVB-1680 is an advanced evaluation board powered by the AstraSOM-1680 module, ideal for prototyping AI and computer vision applications in IoT, smart homes, and industrial automation. It features multi-camera support, robust connectivity, and reliable performance in various lighting conditions, making it suitable for 3D modeling and autonomous systems.

Grinn GenioSOM-700
------------------

Grinn GenioEVB-700 is a feature-rich evaluation board designed to demonstrate the capabilities of the GenioSOM-700 module, streamlining development of AI, IoT, and multimedia applications. It supports rapid prototyping with advanced debugging tools, Raspberry Pi HAT compatibility, and versatile interfaces for displays, cameras, and wireless modules.
